About Me

Hey, I'm Joshua Butt — I edit commercials for a living, and I love what I do.

I grew up in New Jersey, went to film school in Florida, and for the past ten years, I've been based in Los Angeles, cutting stories for brands like Walmart, Behr, Verizon, and Nintendo. Big names, big campaigns — but what keeps me hooked is the creative process.

Editing, for me, is all about feel. It's about knowing when to hold a moment, when to make the cut, and how to shape footage into something that actually says something. Something that sticks.

Sometimes you get the perfect shot, and sometimes you don't — and that's where I come in. I love working with what's there, finding the heartbeat of a project, and building something that just works.

If you're into stories that move, let's talk.
